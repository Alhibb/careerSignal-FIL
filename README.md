## üìñ Overview

**CareerSignal On-Chain** is a decentralized protocol that empowers professionals to build a sovereign, verifiable record of their career achievements. By leveraging the Filecoin network and the Filecoin Virtual Machine (FVM), we provide the tools to create immutable, transparent, and user-owned proofs of work history, skills, and credentials.

This platform eliminates dependence on centralized services like LinkedIn, giving individuals true ownership of their professional identity and enabling a new layer of trust for recruiters, DAOs, and the entire Web3 ecosystem.

## üéØ The Problem

In today's digital world, our professional identity is fragmented and controlled by third-party platforms.
*   **Centralized Control:** Platforms can censor, alter, or revoke access to your data at any time.
*   **Lack of Verifiability:** It's difficult to prove that a credential or skill listed on a profile is authentic.
*   **Data Silos:** Career history is locked into specific platforms, making it hard to build a universal, interoperable resume.
*   **Inefficiency for Web3:** DAOs and decentralized companies lack trust-minimized tools to vet talent and verify contributions.

## ‚ú® The Solution

CareerSignal On-Chain solves this by moving professional identity onto a decentralized, user-owned foundation.

*   **Verifiable Credentials:** Smart contracts on the FVM allow for the minting of career milestones (certificates, project completions, skill endorsements) as on-chain assets (NFTs).
*   **Decentralized & Persistent Storage:** All associated portfolio data (project files, certificate PDFs, resumes) is stored immutably on **Filecoin** via **IPFS**, ensuring it remains accessible and tamper-proof for the long term.
*   **Developer-First Ecosystem:** A robust **SDK and API** will allow any dApp, job board, or DAO to seamlessly integrate CareerSignal's verification features, enriching the entire Filecoin developer ecosystem.
